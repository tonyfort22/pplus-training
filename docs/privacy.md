# PPLUS Training Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

## Overview

PPLUS Training is a coach-led training app for hockey athletes. We collect only the information needed to create accounts, assign training, log workouts, show progress, handle invitations, and provide support.

## Information we collect

- Account details such as name, email address, role, and sign-in information.
- Athlete profile details such as position, height, weight, date of birth, and profile photo when provided.
- Training data such as assigned programs, workouts, exercises, logged sets, progress, and completion history.
- Coach and athlete relationship data needed to manage invitations, athlete lists, and training assignments.
- Support requests and messages submitted through PPLUS Training support channels.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Provide and maintain athlete and coach accounts.
- Show training plans, workout history, progress, and exercise media.
- Let coaches invite and manage athletes.
- Respond to support requests and troubleshoot app issues.
- Improve app reliability, safety, and user experience.

## Health and fitness information

Workout, progress, and training-load information is used to show training activity and help coaches and athletes track completed work. PPLUS Training is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent injuries or medical conditions.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the app, such as authentication, database hosting, file storage, email delivery, and support tooling. Coaches can see information for athletes connected to their account.

## Account deletion and support

To request account deletion or ask data questions, contact us at anthony.fortugno@performeplus.com. We may need to verify the request before deleting account data.

In the mobile app, users can start this request from Profile → Legal → Delete Account.

## Changes

We may update this policy as PPLUS Training changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.
